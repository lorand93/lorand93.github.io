How does it work?

Do you want to have a great meal at a best price?

We can show you the nearby food partners that offer discounts up to 80% before they close the store. With Time to Eat you can buy great food a the lowest price before the store closes.
  • Delicious Food
    With Time to Eat you can buy food that remains unsold before the store closes.
  • The lowest price
    Find food offers at the lowest price in your town!
  • Fight food waste
    Right now 40% of the produces food is being thrown away. Using Time to Eat you can fight against food waste.
  • A greener planet
    By using Time to Eat you actively contribute at protecting the planet, all this while enjoying a great meal.

How it works?

Choose Food

Download the Time to Eat app and choose one of the available offers from nearby locals.

Send the order

After you finish your order you can go pick it up until the shop's closing hour. You can pay using any payment method the shop accepts.

Pick up the food

Smile! You just contributed to the well-being of our planet while enjoying a delightful meal at the lowest price!

Easy to use

Time to Eat Applications help you find food offers close to your location.

Partner's list

With Time to Eat you find the closest new local shops. We work to expand our partners network everyday.

Closing hour

You can pick up the order in the interval specified by the restaurant. The orders must be picked up until the closing hour.

Big discounts!

The discounts can get so big that it's almost free. Keep an eye out for the biggest promotion on Time to Eat.

Distance to the Local

You can see where are the locals you can order from and get directions on how to get there from your location. The nearest locations are displayed first.
